Privacy Policy

Last modified: January 5, 2026

We wrote this to be clear and readable.

Quick summary

  • We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party ads.
  • There are no user accounts on this Website.
  • The assessment is meant to run in your browser. Unless you choose to contact us, we do not ask you to submit your identity.
  • Like most sites, limited technical data is collected for security and basic site operation.

1) What information we collect

A. Information you choose to send us

If you contact us (for example by email), we will receive what you send, such as your message content and your email address.

B. Assessment answers and results

The assessment is designed to run locally in your browser. We do not require you to enter your name, email, or other direct identifiers to view your result.

If you voluntarily send us your answers or result (for example in a support email), then we will receive that information because you chose to share it.

C. Technical information collected automatically

When you visit the Website, technical data may be collected automatically to help the Website run safely and reliably. This can include:

  • IP address
  • browser type and device details
  • date/time of requests
  • pages viewed and basic usage logs

This data is commonly captured in server and security logs.

2) Cookies and local storage

A. What we use

The Website may use cookies or local storage that are needed for basic function, such as:

  • remembering language choice
  • keeping simple site settings
  • storing your assessment progress or result on your device

B. What we do not use

We do not use cookies for third-party ad targeting.

C. Your control

You can delete cookies and site storage at any time using your browser settings. If you do, parts of the Website may reset (for example, saved settings or a saved result on your device).

3) How we use information

We use information only as needed to:

  • operate and maintain the Website
  • keep the Website secure and prevent abuse
  • respond to messages you send us
  • improve the Website (for example, fixing bugs and improving clarity)

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data only in the following cases:

  • Service providers that host and deliver the Website (they may process technical logs needed to provide the service)
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security
